What we do

What we aspire

AIMP (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Perception) LABS aims at making research more mainstream in and around Kolkata. Currently the Lab engages in the development of a local ecosystem for facilitating research and technology development. As a research based organization AIMP has a long term vision of conducting high-impact projects, promoting future researchers, and creating technologies for the future.
