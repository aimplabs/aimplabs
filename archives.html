Professional Development Programs
The primary and perhaps the sole objective of our training program is to address the skill deficit of the entering workforce in the field of artificial intelligence and machine learning with a particular emphasis on visual technologies. College and universities in India have the responsibility to build up fundamentals and they don't always have the bandwidth to cater to the industrial grade skill demand. On top of that, the area of artificial intelligence has been moving at a rapid pace in the past couple of years.

What AIMP LABS offers is a hands-on training program with strong emphasis on large scale system development. We take great interest in theory; not only that it helps us to become better engineers but also because we subscribe to the notion that great theory comes from novel cases in practice. Our core tech-stack comprises Python, C and C++ (yes, C and C++ are different languages). We assume that the students come with some prior exposure to any programming language, i.e., (s)he should be conversant with loops and conditional control structure with at least some exposure to file handling. Some of the advanced course may require deeper skill set.
