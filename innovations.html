Recently We have developed an advanced AI-powered chatbot designed to interact with and interpret the three recently enacted Indian laws:
  • BNS (Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita) - Replacing the Indian Penal Code (IPC), it governs criminal offenses.
  • BNSS (Bharatiya Nagrik Suraksha Sanhita) - Replacing the Criminal Procedure Code (CrPC), it outlines procedures for criminal investigations and trials.
  • BSA (Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam) - Replacing the Indian Evidence Act, it governs the rules of evidence in legal proceedings.
    • Users can query specific sections, clauses, and legal terminologies for clear and accurate explanations. The chatbot provides contextual insights by referencing relevant sections across the three laws. Designed for legal professionals, law students, and citizens seeking quick and reliable legal information.
