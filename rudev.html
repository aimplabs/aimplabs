AIMP Labs aims at making research more mainstream in and around Kolkata. Currently the Lab engages in the development of a local ecosystem for facilitating research and the technology development process in the field of computer vision and visual technologies, our core strength and expertise. As a research based organization, AIMP has a long term vision of conducting high-impact research, promoting future researchers, and creating technologies for the future.

If you are interested in engaging with us we recommend you to consider one or more of the following ways to stay connected.
Follow us on Twitter, Github
Follow us on twitter to know about our current engagements, projects and news.

Attend the Webinars/Workshops (visit the websites)
Also, keep checking our training page for webinars/workshops.

Enroll in courses
We announce courses in our training page as well as on social media.

Engage in research collaboration
Do you wish to pick our brain for anything related to AI? Email us!
